Ubbalamadugu Falls

Thiruvallur

Description:


Ubbalamadugu Falls, famously known as Tada Falls, in Thiruvallur, Tamil Nadu, is an exhilarating natural marvel! This stunning cascade of water, set amidst breathtaking surroundings, is an absolute delight for nature enthusiasts! As the water rushes down the rocky cliffs, it creates a captivating spectacle that will leave you in awe! The name "Tada Falls" is derived from the town of Tada, where the falls are located. Embark on an adventure to witness the mesmerizing beauty of Tada Falls and let the cascading waters enchant your senses!
