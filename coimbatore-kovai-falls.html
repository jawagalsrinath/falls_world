Kovai Kutralam Falls

Coimbatore

Description:


Kovai Kutralam Falls, also known as Siruvani Waterfalls, is a breathtaking gem near Coimbatore in Tamil Nadu! Formed by the majestic Siruvani River, this 30-foot waterfall cascades down rocky cliffs, enveloped by lush greenery and a mesmerizing misty ambiance! It's an absolute paradise for a refreshing dip and immersing yourself in the unparalleled beauty of the Western Ghats! Make sure to visit during the monsoon season for an awe-inspiring experience that will leave you in sheer awe!
